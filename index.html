About NBA 2K17

NBA 2K17 is a basketball video game released in 2016 by Visual Concepts and published by 2K. It features realistic gameplay, updated rosters, MyCareer mode, and online play. The game was known for its realism and is still considered one of the best NBA 2K games ever made.

In NBA 2K17, you can build a player, play through a full season, and compete against players all over the world. The controls are smooth and the animations feel realistic.

Key Features

Realistic Gameplay

Shooting, dribbling, and defending require timing and skill. Every player feels different and has their own style.

MyCareer Mode

In MyCareer, you create a custom player and guide them from a rookie to an NBA star. You can upgrade your character and play online.

Classic Teams

NBA 2K17 includes classic teams like the 1996 Chicago Bulls and many more legends.
